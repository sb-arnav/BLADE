    - **WIRED-NOT-USED** — UI surface exists (route/button/command-palette entry) but the intended backend call is missing or unreachable
    - **NOT-WIRED** — module exposes `#[tauri::command]`(s) AND/OR emits events, BUT no `invokeTyped` / `useTauriEvent` subscriber exists in `src/`, AND no internal Rust caller references it
    - **DEAD** — no `#[tauri::command]`, no event emissions used by any subscriber, no `crate::<name>::` callers anywhere in `src-tauri/src/`, AND not referenced in `body_registry.rs`, AND not mentioned in `.planning/REQUIREMENTS.md` as planned for v1.1
