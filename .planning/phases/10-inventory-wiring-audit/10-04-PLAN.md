Session count expected: 1 feature (`local-whisper`).

    **Classification rules (per D-48):**

    - **ACTIVE** — field has a UI surface in `src/features/settings/` OR is core to startup (e.g. primary provider key, DB path)
    - **WIRED-NOT-USED** — field is read at runtime but no UI surface exposes it (most env vars fall here; also fields in BladeConfig but not DiskConfig per Pitfall 8)
    - **NOT-WIRED** — field exists in struct but nothing reads it AND nothing persists it
    - **DEAD** — field exists in struct but nothing reads it anywhere

    **Pitfall 8 enforcement:** a field present in `BladeConfig` but NOT in `DiskConfig` MUST be emitted with `disk_persisted: false` and `classification: WIRED-NOT-USED` and a `notes: "6-place rule gap; present in BladeConfig but not DiskConfig — add to DiskConfig in Phase 14"`.
